# ERR_NGROK_415

### Message

Your account is limited to `MAX` reserved domains. If you need more domains added, you can either turn overages on for your existing account (in billing-->subscriptions) or you can subscribe to usage-based billing with the Advanced plan. 

### Further help

If you're having trouble resolving this error, please reach out to [support@ngrok.com](mailto:support@ngrok.com?subject=Help%20with%20ERR_NGROK_415)
